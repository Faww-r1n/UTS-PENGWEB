Gameplay Fate/Grand Order

Fate/Grand Order is a mobile gacha game developed by Delightworks and published by Aniplex. In this game, players take on the role of a "Master" who summons various characters known as "Servants" to battle in turn-based combat.

Main Gameplay Features

Combat Mechanism

Combat occurs in a turn-based format, where players can choose combinations of attack cards to strike enemies. Each Servant has three types of attacks: Quick, Arts, and Buster. Players must choose wisely to maximize damage.

Gacha and Servant Collection

Players can obtain Servants through a gacha system using in-game currency. Servants have different rarity levels, ranging from 1 to 5 stars.

Leveling and Customization

Servants can be leveled up by gaining experience and using certain materials. Players can also equip "CE" (Craft Essences) that provide buffs and enhance Servants' stats.

Events and Storyline

This game frequently hosts special events with unique storylines and limited-time Servants. The game's story follows engaging plots based on various myths and histories.

Multiplayer

Although the main gameplay is solo, players can send their Servants to assist others in quests.
